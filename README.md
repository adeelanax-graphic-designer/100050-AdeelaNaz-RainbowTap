# 🌈 RainbowTap - Interactive Experience

**RainbowTap** is a vibrant and multi-purpose Android application designed to provide entertainment, creativity, and daily motivation. Whether you want to test your reflexes, create beautiful quote cards, or track your daily progress, RainbowTap has it all in one colorful package.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎮 Gaming
*   **Fast Tap Challenge**: A high-energy speed test! Tap the target as many times as you can in 10 seconds and earn ranks from "Beginner" to "Legendary."
*   **Rhythm Matcher**: A musical beat-matching game. The colors pulse to the rhythm, and the speed increases as your score grows. How fast can you keep up?

### 🎨 Creativity & Tools
*   **Quote Card Maker**: Create personalized quote cards. Enter your favorite title and quote, choose a background color (Red, Blue, or Green), and generate a beautiful card instantly.
*   **Color Mixer**: Explore the world of colors by mixing different shades to create unique visual experiences.

### 💡 Motivation
*   **Random Quote Generator**: Get a dose of inspiration with a single click. Generate random motivational quotes and share them directly with your friends via social media.

### 📈 Personal Progress
*   **User Profile**: Keep track of your journey. Monitor your **Daily Streaks**, **Total Points** earned from games, and unlock special **Achievements**.

---

## 🛠️ Built With
*   **Language**: Java
*   **UI/UX**: XML (Material Design, CardViews, Custom Gradients)
*   **Backend**: Android SharedPreferences (for local data tracking)
*   **IDE**: Android Studio
